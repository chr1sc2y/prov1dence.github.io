VGG-16

Process
  • input: 224 224 3 --> Conv64 ( 2): 224 224 * 64
  • --> MaxPool: 112 112 64
  • --> Conv128 ( 2): 112 112 * 128
  • --> MaxPool: 56 56 128
  • --> Conv256 ( 3): 56 56 * 256
  • --> MaxPool: 28 28 256
  • --> Conv512 ( 3): 28 28 * 512
  • --> MaxPool: 14 14 512
  • --> Conv512 ( 3): 14 14 * 512
  • --> MaxPool: 7 7 512
  • --> FC: 4096
  • --> FC: 4096
  • --> Softmax: 1000
Pros
  • the rate of shrinking images and increasing channels is systematic

Inception

1 * 1 Convolution

  • traverse all positions with a 1 * 1 filter
  • shrink the channel when large
    • from n_c to n_f
  • save computation
  • learn more the complex function of your network
  • as bottleneck layer
